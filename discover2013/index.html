    Session Description

    Process automation is the key to running IT operations as a business by providing repeatable, measurable and higher-quality services whilst reducing cost. However, many organizations face challenges implementing their automation projects. Join us and learn how some of your peers have mitigated risk and exceeded expectations by implementing an Automation Center of Excellence to ensure their success.
